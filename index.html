Flint Water Crisis

General Overview

The premise of the website is to thoroughly explain the cause of the Flint Water Crisis and elements that sustained the crisis for years. This entails explaining the people and institutions involved in denying the legitimacy of the crisis and ensuring that there wasn’t an adequate or timely response to the problem. To elaborate, the Flint water crisis in Michigan ultimately began when the city decided to move from its traditional drinking water supply to the Flint river in efforts to save money. However, there was a poor amount of testing, leading to severe health issues from the insufficient and contaminated water supply. The disturbance caused massive concern from the people of Flint, which ultimately was overlooked and discredited by government officials. The contaminated water caused most people throughout the population to come down with major health issues, also causing increased levels of lead in the individuals bloodstreams. This was directly due to the large amounts of lead and other contaminants in the water supply. We will emphasize the government’s poor guidance and management in regards to mitigating the harms of the crisis, and also briefly go over how the crisis came to an end after doctors and scientists collaborated to determine the affliction the drinking water had caused.
